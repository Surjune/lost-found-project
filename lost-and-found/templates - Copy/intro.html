Sri Krishna Arts and Science College

Welcome to the official Lost & Found Portal for Sri Krishna Arts and Science College – your trusted gateway to recovering lost belongings on campus with ease and security.

This platform is designed specifically for our college community of students, faculty, and staff. Here, you can:

  • Report lost or found items quickly using simple forms with detailed descriptions and images.
  • Search and browse current lost & found listings easily from your phone or computer.
  • Connect safely with finders or owners to arrange item recovery at a convenient time and place.
  • Enjoy peace of mind as all reports are reviewed and verified by trusted admins for authenticity and to prevent misuse.

Our goal is to minimize the disruption caused by lost belongings by making recovery straightforward, encouraging community responsibility, and fostering trust across campus.
